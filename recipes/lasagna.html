Lasagna

Description

Recipe is very simple and easy to be followed. All you have to do is follow along. Be extremely careful in following the steps as they're beyond important if you want your lasagna to look as delicious as the one in the photo!

Ingredients:

  • 150g Mozzarella
  • 100g Ricotta cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 20g Basil
  • 100g Tomato paste
  • 250g Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 bags of Pasta

Steps:

  1. Cut the onion
  2. Heat it in a pan after adding 2 oil drops
  3. Add 100g of tomato to the mixture on the oven
  4. Add 2 spoons of salt & pepper then mix
  5. Add 2 cups of boiling water
  6. Add your pasta
  7. Leave fot 30 minutes
  8. Remove the pan from the oven after turning off
  9. Add your mixture in a tray
  10. Add the meatballs, mozzarella, basil, parmesan and ricotta cheese
  11. Heat the oven on 180 degrees and leave tray inside for an hour
