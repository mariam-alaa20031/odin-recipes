Brownies

Description

Recipe is very simple and easy to be followed. All you have to do is follow along. Be extremely careful in following the steps as they're beyond important if you want your brownies to look as fudgy as the one in the photo!

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 200g white sugar
  • 200g cocoa powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tsp vanilla
  • 200g butter

Steps:

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Add 200g of sugar
  3. Crack 3 eggs and 3 vanilla
  4. Mix
  5. Add the flour
  6. LAdd the cocoa
  7. Mix
  8. Add your mixture in a tray
  9. Heat the oven on 180 degrees and leave tray inside for 25 minutes
