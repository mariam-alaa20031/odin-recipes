Chicken Sweet & Sour

Description

Recipe is very simple and easy to be followed. All you have to do is follow along. Be extremely careful in following the steps as they're beyond important if you want your sweet and sour chicken look as delectable as the one in the photo!

Ingredients:

  • 200g chicken breast pieces
  • 200g sugar
  • 1/2 cup soya sauce
  • 3 spoons barbecue
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken pieces
  2. Add salt & pepper
  3. Add sugar on heated pan with chicken
  4. Mix
  5. Add soya sauce
  6. Add the barbecue
  7. Mix
